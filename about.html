About

Hi there! I'm Seven, yes like the number, but you can call me Sev. I'm 30 and I live the Twin Cities metro area of Minnesota with my partner and our three cats, Mulder, Bast, and Jolly.

Since then, I've continued writing in my free time as much as I can.
